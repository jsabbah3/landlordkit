## Description
LandlordKit is a suite of free calculators and document generators for
independent landlords. Every state-aware tool — security deposit interest,
late-fee caps, rent-increase notices, deposit-return deadlines — cites the
actual statute and shows the date the rule was last verified. Tools run
entirely in your browser (your numbers never leave your device), need no
signup, and export polished PDFs. There's also a personalized compliance
calendar that builds your tax/filing deadlines and exports to your calendar.
Pro ($12/mo or $99/yr) adds saved properties, batch document generation,
watermark-free branded PDFs, lease-PDF autofill, and deadline reminders pushed
to your calendar.

What's different here:

• The legal data lives in auditable files I update as laws change

Every tool is free without limits. Pro ($12/mo) adds the convenience layer:
saved properties, batch receipts, watermark-free branding, lease-PDF autofill,
and a compliance calendar that reminds you before each filing deadline. Would
love feedback — especially from landlords: which rule does your state get wrong
on other sites?
