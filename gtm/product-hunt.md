## Description
LandlordKit is a suite of free calculators and document generators for
independent landlords. Every state-aware tool — security deposit interest,
late-fee caps, rent-increase notices, deposit-return deadlines — cites the
actual statute and shows the date the rule was last verified. Tools run
entirely in your browser (your numbers never leave your device), need no
signup, and export polished PDFs. Pro ($12/mo) adds saved properties, batch
documents, and custom branding.

## First comment (founder)
Hey PH 👋 I'm Jake. I built LandlordKit because every "free" landlord tool I
found was either an ad farm, a lead-capture wall, or just wrong about state
law — and landlord-tenant rules genuinely differ in all 50 states (only ~15
require deposit interest; late-fee caps range from 4% to none at all).

What's different here:
• Every state rule cites its statute + a last-verified date
• Calculators run client-side — your financials never hit a server
• PDFs download instantly, no account
• The legal data lives in auditable files I update as laws change

All 8 tools are free without limits. Pro adds convenience (saved properties,
batch receipts, your branding on PDFs). Would love feedback — especially from
landlords: which rule does your state get wrong on other sites?
