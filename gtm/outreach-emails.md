# Blogger / newsletter outreach — 5 ready-to-send emails

**Targets:** landlord-tenant law blogs, personal-finance sites with rental
content, landlord newsletters/podcasts, property-management content teams, and
REI newsletters. Find them: google `landlord blog`, `landlord newsletter`,
`"security deposit laws" blog 2026`, check who ranks for your target keywords.

**Rules:** short, specific, zero flattery-spam. Lead with what's in it for
their readers. One clear ask. Send from your real address with your name.

---

## 1. The "resource for your article" (to anyone ranking for deposit/late-fee topics)
**Subject:** A statute-cited deposit-interest table for your [article title] piece

Hi [Name] — your piece on [topic] comes up top when landlords search this, so
this might be useful for your next refresh: I maintain a free, statute-cited
database of security-deposit interest rules (only ~15 states including DC
require it; rates run from ~1% in Minnesota to 5% in Massachusetts), plus
deposit-return deadlines for all 50 states, each with the statute and a
last-verified date.

Feel free to cite any of it — happy to send the full table, or there are free
state-by-state calculators your readers can use directly:
getlandlordkit.com/tools/security-deposit-interest-calculator

— Jake, founder of LandlordKit (solo project; the data files are public)

getlandlordkit.com/tools/late-fee-calculator”

Happy to write a short blurb in your voice if that helps. — Jake
